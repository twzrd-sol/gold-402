# Community

Where x402 builders hang out, get help, and find work.

---

## Official Channels

- [x402 Foundation Discord](https://discord.gg/x402) — Official community server. Best place for protocol questions and ecosystem announcements.
- [GitHub Issues — coinbase/x402](https://github.com/coinbase/x402/issues) — Technical Q&A, bug reports, and feature requests for the core protocol.
- [Twitter @x402org](https://twitter.com/x402org) — Official updates and announcements.

---

## Developer Communities

- [x402 Builders Telegram](https://t.me/x402builders) — Active developer chat. Good for quick questions and sharing what you're building.
- [Reddit r/x402](https://reddit.com/r/x402) — Community forum and discussions.
- [Dev.to #x402](https://dev.to/t/x402) — Tutorials and builder articles. Good for longer-form content.
- [Farcaster x402 Channel](https://warpcast.com/~/channel/x402) — Decentralized social channel for x402 builders.

---

## Newsletters

- [Agent Economy Digest](https://agenteconomy.substack.com) — AI agent payments news covering x402, MPP, A2A, and the broader agentic commerce stack.
- x402 Weekly — Protocol updates (via official x402 channels).

## Jobs & Career

- [24K Labs Jobs](https://24klabs.ai/community/jobs) — x402 ecosystem job board. Developer, DevRel, and infrastructure roles across the x402 ecosystem.
- [Human Pages](https://humanpages.ai) — Open directory for AI agents to hire humans for real-world tasks. x402 pay-per-use on Base. Also works for humans looking for gigs.
- [CryptoJobsList](https://cryptojobslist.com) — Crypto and Web3 job board with growing x402/agent economy listings.
- [WorkProtocol](https://workprotocol.ai) — Structured work marketplace for AI agents and the humans building them. Escrow-backed jobs, on-chain reputation.
